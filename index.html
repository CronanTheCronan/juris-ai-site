The intake problem

Before strategy, there is the mess.

Clients send screenshots, PDFs, emails, photos, notes, recordings, partial timelines, duplicate files, and missing context. Someone has to turn that pile into something an attorney can actually review.

01

Scattered materials

Evidence arrives across formats, folders, screenshots, forwarded emails, and client memory.

02

Hidden chronology

Key dates are buried inside documents, message threads, attachments, and disconnected notes.

03

Expensive review time

Attorneys and staff burn hours just figuring out what exists, what matters, and what is missing.

The focused wedge

The AI never decides the case. It prepares the mess so the attorney can decide faster.

Juris-AI is not a lawyer replacement. It is a case-material intelligence layer: a cleaner way to see the timeline, evidence, contradictions, and follow-up questions hiding inside a messy case folder.
